The Rastha

Reel path. Cinema soul.

ReelRastha isn't a review page. It's a running argument with cinema — loud, opinionated, and allergic to star ratings. No single industry gets the spotlight; if a film exists, it's fair game.

The name does double duty on purpose: reel for the medium, rastha — path, or road — for the journey every film takes you on. Desi at its core, wide open in its taste.

"We yell about cinema into the void. Turns out the void yells back."
Reel The cinema. The craft. The frame.
Rastha Hindi / Urdu for path, road, journey.
Together A cinematic road trip, one opinion at a time.
